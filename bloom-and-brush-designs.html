The Process

How it works

From your first inquiry to hanging the finished piece on your wall, here's what to expect.

01

Get in Touch

Meet the Artist

Hi, I'm Bri.

I'm a watercolor artist (and therapist by day) based in Bloomington, Indiana. I picked up a brush in 2016 looking for a creative outlet and completely fell in love with the unpredictable, beautiful nature of watercolor.

What started with florals has grown into a full passion for live painting. There's something magical about creating art in the moment, capturing the energy of a wedding reception or the laughter at a paint night. I love being part of people's stories.

When I'm not painting, you can find me being a mom, knitting, doing puzzles, or going to bed by 9pm. (I'm a proud old lady at heart.)
